# Excel Dashboard Project – Zepto Data Analysis & Visualization  
---

##  **Project Overview**  
The goal of this project was to analyze Zepto’s sales performance across multiple outlets and identify the key factors influencing revenue, customer behavior, and product performance.  

This project helped me strengthen my **Data cleaning**, **Dashboard design**, and **Insight Communication** skills using Excel.

---

##  **Project Workflow**

### 1️ Data Cleaning  
- Removed null and duplicate values.  
- Standardized data formats (dates, categories, and numbers).  
- Ensured consistency for accurate KPI calculation and visualization.  

### 2️ KPI Creation  
Key metrics were derived to get a quick snapshot of overall performance:  
| KPI | Value | Insight |
|------|--------|----------|
|  **Total Sales** | **$1.20M** | Indicates strong overall business performance |
|  **Items Sold** | **8,523** | Reflects high product turnover |
|  **Avg. Sales per Transaction** | **$141** | Suggests efficient sales per order |
|  **Avg. Product Rating** | **4.0** | Shows steady customer satisfaction |

---

##  **Dashboard Insights**

### 1️ Total Sales by Fat Content  
 **Donut Chart** – Shows how low, regular, and high-fat items contribute to total sales.  
➡ Helps identify which fat category performs best in revenue generation.  

### 2️ Fat Content by Outlet  
 **Stacked Column Chart** – Compares fat content distribution across different outlets.  
➡ Useful for evaluating outlet-level performance by product type.  

### 3️ Sales by Item Type  
 **Bar Chart** – Displays sales by product categories such as Fruits & Vegetables, Dairy, Snacks, Beverages, etc.  
➡ Helps identify customer preferences and best-selling categories.  

### 4️ Sales by Outlet Establishment Year  
 **Line Chart** – Tracks performance by outlet launch year.  
➡ Highlights how newly established outlets are scaling over time.  

### 5️ Sales by Outlet Size  
**Pie Chart** – Breaks down total sales by outlet size (Small, Medium, Large).  
➡ Provides insight into how store capacity impacts revenue.  

### 6️ Sales by Outlet Location  
 **Bar Chart** – Compares sales across various cities or regions.  
➡ Enables location-based sales strategy planning.  

### 7️ Overall Metrics by Outlet Type  
**Grouped Bar Chart** – Shows Total Sales, Average Sales, and Items Sold for each outlet type (Supermarket, Grocery Store, etc.).  
➡ Makes it easy to compare which outlet type performs the best.
